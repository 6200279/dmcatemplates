# DMCA Takedown Notice Templates - Priority 1 (Essential)

**Legal Compliance**: All templates comply with 17 U.S.C. ยง 512(c)(3)(A) and include mandatory elements established by case law (Lenz v. Universal Music, MFB Fertility v. Action Care).

**Important Legal Warning**: 
- Sending false DMCA notices can result in liability under 17 U.S.C. ยง 512(f)
- Perjury statements carry potential criminal penalties
- You must consider fair use AND copyrightability before filing
- All contact information will be shared with alleged infringers

## Legal Compliance Checklist

Before sending ANY DMCA notice, verify:

- [ ] **Copyrightability Assessment Completed**
  - Work is original creative expression
  - Work is fixed in tangible form
  - Work does not consist solely of unprotectable elements

- [ ] **Fair Use Analysis Conducted**
  - Evaluated purpose/character of use
  - Considered nature of copyrighted work
  - Assessed amount/substantiality used
  - Analyzed market effect

- [ ] **All Six Statutory Elements Present**
  1. Physical or electronic signature
  2. Identification of copyrighted work
  3. Identification and location of infringing material
  4. Contact information (address, phone, email)
  5. Good faith belief statement
  6. Accuracy statement under penalty of perjury

- [ ] **Accuracy Verification**
  - URLs are correct and currently active
  - Dates are accurate
  - Claims are truthful
  - Evidence supports claims

- [ ] **Privacy Awareness**
  - Understand contact info will be shared with infringer
  - Using agent/attorney if privacy is concern
  - Prepared for potential counter-notice
